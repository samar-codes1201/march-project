March 20 · The most important reason of all

So. You're another year older. Congratulations on surviving 365 more days of me — that alone deserves a medal, an award, and probably some sort of government recognition.

I've been trying to write this for a while now, and every draft ended up sounding either like a wedding vow or a LinkedIn recommendation, so we're just going to go with honest.

The truth is — I don't know what I did to deserve someone like you. You listen to me when I overthink everything. You hug me until the world gets quiet. You look at me like I'm something worth looking at, on the days I feel like absolutely nothing.

You completely destroy bhangra with full confidence. You cry over spicy ramen and somehow still look cute doing it. You steal my T-shirts and pull them off better than I ever could. Honestly, it's rude. I want my clothes back. Actually no, keep them.

You've been beside me in different cities, across distance, through all of it — and you never made me feel like it was hard. You made it feel like home. That's a kind of love I didn't know existed before you.

Every single day this month, I wrote you a reason. But honestly? There are too many to count. Every laugh, every hug, every time you yell "Go bb" while I'm dying at the gym — it all adds up to something that feels bigger than any word I know.

You are the person I want beside me for every ordinary Tuesday, every stupid argument we resolve in twenty minutes, every late-night call, every biryani run, every everything.

Also — you owe me a camel face performance for my birthday. It's only fair.

Happy Birthday, Mariyam. The world got so lucky the day you arrived in it. And I got the luckiest of all — because somehow, you chose me too.

Yours, always & completely 💜
